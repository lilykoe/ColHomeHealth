Insurance Coverage

Columbine Home Health, Inc. is a Medicare and Medicaid Certified agency. Many private insurers cover Home Health Care, and we accept Private Pay as well. These services are affordable and can include sliding-fee scale services based on income.



Grant Support

Our services are made possible by grants from Aspen Community Foundation, The Daniels Fund, Garfield County, The Helen K. and Arthur E. Johnson Foundation, Northwest Options for Long Term Care, 100+ Women Who Care: Roaring Fork Valley and The Thrift Shop of Aspen.
